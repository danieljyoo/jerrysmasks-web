Why donate?

Many small but essential organizations are unable to source and purchase high quality masks. They lack access and the ability to vet overseas manufacturers and the budgets to make high volume orders. A small donation of high quality masks can make a big difference.

How to Donate

1
Find an organization in need
 

There are likely to be organizations in need of masks in your own local community. Your personal network of friends and family may also know of organizations in need of masks or may have a personal need themselves.

Resources and Tips

  • Email template you can send to friends and family.
  • Outreach email template you can use to contact organizations.
  • Organizations nearby you who likely are in critical need for masks.
  • National efforts that are seeking to distribute masks.

2
Contact and confirm details
 

One you've identified an organization in need you will need to get additional information from them in order to make a successful donation.

  • Shipping address
  • Email and phone number of an individual in the organization
  • Confirmation they are seeking and will use donated masks

Resources and Tips

  • Call script you can use to contact and get all necessary details.
  • Email template you can use to get an organization's information.
  • You may also want to ask if they are a registered 501(c)(3) organization and whether your donation will be tax-deductible.

3
Purchase and ship directly
 

Use the Donate order form below to purchase and ship your masks. Ensure that you've entered the correct name and address of the donee for the shipping address before making payment.

In addition to your own private receipt and shipping notification, you will receive a shareable receipt and shipping confirmation. You can choose to forward these emails to the organization you are donating to so that they have a written record of your purchase and can track their shipment on their own.

We do not initiate any communication with the organization you are supporting nor do we use their contact information for any purpose except to ship the order.

  • Organizations nearby you who likely are in critical need for masks.
  • National efforts that are seeking to distribute masks.

About Us

We are a few friends who are working together across borders to make high quality masks more accessible for all people, especially for the most vulnerable and the people who care for them. We believe that people having access to high quality masks is critical in the fight against Coronavirus.

We work on a strictly voluntary basis and will not receive any financial compensation for this work. Proceeds will fund donations and supplying more masks for those in great need.

Jerry Huang (Shenzhen, China) LinkedIn
