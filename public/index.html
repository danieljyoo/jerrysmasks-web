FFP3 3D Isolation Mask

Breathable viral barrier face mask made with lightweight tri-laminate produced with polypropylene spun bond layers surrounding a breathable film that can be used in the construction of surgical gowns and face masks. It has excellent barrier properties especially for critical areas, low lint and is soft, comfortable and breathable. Meets highest criteria level as a barrier against particulate, liquid, and aerosolized infectious agents in all PPE applications. Classifications EN 14126:2003. With CE.
